About COMSATS

COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI) was established in 1998 to promote research and innovation in Pakistan.

  • Founded to enhance technological advancements in Pakistan.
  • Offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs.
  • Recognized for its research contributions at an international level.
  • Hosts international conferences and workshops regularly.
  • Committed to producing future leaders and innovators.

Library

  • Access to online research databases and digital libraries.
  • Separate reading halls for undergraduate and postgraduate students.
  • 24/7 study rooms available for students.
  • Dedicated sections for research and thesis work.
  • Expert librarians for guidance on research material.

Advanced Laboratories

  • State-of-the-art computer labs with the latest software.
  • Well-equipped physics, chemistry, and engineering labs.
  • Dedicated AI and robotics labs for research.
  • High-speed internet and cloud computing access for students.
  • Project-based learning environment for practical exposure.
